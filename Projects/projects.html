Bachelor Thesis - Applying Methods of Game Feel

2019 - Grade 1.3

Why are some games more fun than others? A very common answer to this question is that some games have better gameplay. It is true that at the base of every good game is good gameplay, but gameplay is not something completely independent of all the other things in a videogame. For example, would Super Mario 64 still be so much fun, if instead of controlling Mario with all his animations and sound effects, you would control a rectangular box?

Need For Sheep

2018 - 4. Place at TUM Semester Game Jam

This game was created for the 2018 Winter Semester Game Jam at the Technical University of Munich. It was a 48-Hour Jam, which forces you to work very fast. The topic of the gamejam was "Discovery" which was something very different to the usual topics. We decided to create a game where you play as a shepherd who has to discover/find the wolf amongst the sheep.

Flight

2018 - Out on Google Play Store

Flight was a project which I basically did just out of curiosity. All I wanted to do was to publish a finished game on the app store, which I did. Granted it is really nothing special but I am still proud to be able to say that you can download one of my games. All you do in this little pixel game is to dodge asteroids, which fly at you at ever faster speeds. The controls are very simple and you can't even unlock anything. It's really all about the highscore.
