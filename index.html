WHO I AM

Hi, my name is Robin and I'm a 23 year old game creator & programmer, which I have learned at the Technical University of Munich. Originally I wanted to study Media Design, but after I got rejected I quickly realized that I'm not that great of an artist. I then remembered that I always loved games and was always interested in how they are created, because to me it seemed like it was magic. So I started to study Games Engineering and quickly fell in love with the art of programming and creating games. Ever since then I have been making games whenever I could, be it for university projects, gamejams or private projects just for fun. This year I finally graduated and am now looking to make my way into the gaming world. My bachelor's thesis was about "Applying Methods of Game Feel" where I created a small platformer. The game was built in a way, which allowed me to modify certain parameters and to test the impact on the feel of the game. What is the feel of a game? That's a good question, but not an easy one to answer. All I can say here is that it is one of the most important aspects of the moment to moment action in a videogame, which is why I was so interested to write about it. You can find the whole thesis here: Bachelor Thesis.
