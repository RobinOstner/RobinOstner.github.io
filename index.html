WHO I AM

Hi, my name is Robin and I'm a 23 year old game creator & programmer. Videogames have always fascinated me and seemed like magic, which is why I started to study Games Engineering at the Technical University of Munich in the summer of 2015. I quickly fell in love with the art of programming and creating games. Ever since then I have been making games whenever I could, be it for university projects, gamejams or private projects just for fun. Last year I finally graduated and am now looking to make my way into the gaming world. This is my portfolio where I showcase a small selection of the things I have done so far. It is the result of studying Games Engineering at the Technical University of Munich for 4 years. Some of the games have been created for 48h GameJams and others are just personal projects, which I have been working on in my spare time.

BACHELOR THESIS

My bachelor's thesis is about "Applying Methods of Game Feel" where I created a small 2D platformer. The game was built in a way, which allowed me to modify certain parameters and to test the impact on the feel of the game. What is the feel of a game? That's a good question, but not an easy one to answer. All I can say here is that it is one of the most important aspects of the moment to moment action in a videogame, which is why I was so interested to write about it. You can find the whole thesis here.
